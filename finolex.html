Features & Benefits

  • Anti-abrasion and anti-corrosion properties ensure long life

Specifications

  • Sizes: 25mm to 100mm (1" to 4") diameter
  • Variants: Coupler type (up to 100mm) and Bell End type (25mm to 40mm)
  • Standard Lengths: 1.5 metres and 3 metres
  • Pressure Ratings: V4, Crystal, Medium, Standard, Heavy, Super Heavy
  • Installation Depth Range: 100 to 400 metres depending on class
  • O-ring Material: EPDM rubber
  • Locking System: Brass riveted — high torque resistance

Key Installation Tips

  • Pump delivery pressure must be less than the selected hydrostatic allowable pressure of column pipes
  • Bore well drilling must be straight down without bending
  • Initial 3 metre GI/steel pipe must be connected directly to pump for heat dissipation during dry run
  • Diameter of tube well must be at least 2"–2.5" more than pump diameter
  • Always mount split clamp on pipe below coupler at the top
  • Tighten pipes by strap wrench — 50% of rubber-sealing ring on male thread gets into coupler seat
  • Do not over-tighten — may crush rubber seals causing leakage
  • Do not apply grease, oil or chemical on threads — use plain or soapy water only
