Jointing Procedure

  • Cut: Cut pipe square using a cutter; de-burr the cut end. Ensure clean, right-angle cuts.
  • Clean: Clean and dry the pipe and fitting surfaces before jointing.
  • Scrub: Scrub the OD of the pipe and ID of fitting with sandpaper and wipe clean with cloth.
  • Apply Solvent Cement: Apply gently and uniformly to scrubbed fitting ID and pipe OD. Apply second coat to pipe OD. Avoid excess cement.
  • Assemble: Within 20 seconds, insert pipe OD into fitting ID and give a quarter turn for uniform distribution. Hold tightly for 15 secs to 3 min depending on size.
  • Cure: Allow minimum 12 hours curing per joint before pressure testing. Longer for cold temperatures.
  • Pressure Test: Must be carried out before concealing pipes.
